About Me

I am a Lecturer in Nursing at the Faculty of Health Sciences, The Open University of Sri Lanka, with a strong foundation in clinical practice, teaching, and research. I am passionate about integrating clinical expertise, research, and education to enhance healthcare outcomes, while actively engaging in scholarly activities that support professional development, academic excellence, and the future growth of the nursing profession.
